Ember.js Workshop

Register now

Learn how to build ambitious Ember.js apps with modern patterns introduced in Ember 2.x.

The Workshop

This 3 day workshop takes participants through building a full Ember.js app (a music player) using modern Ember.js patterns introduced in Ember 2.x like Data down, Actions up, angle bracket components, closure actions etc. The last day will also cover advanced topics like Animations and realtime data with Websockets.

While no previous Ember.js knowledge is required, participants should be familiar with things like JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS and AJAX and the general idea behind MVC.

We also offer diversity scholarships for people in underrepresented groups in the tech community.

Lunch at the Workshop Venue is included in the ticket price and there will be a Welcome Party on the first Evening!
