The Workshops

We organize workshops that teach participants how to build ambitious Ember.js applications using modern and and idiomatic Ember.js patterns.

Our workshops range from beginner trainings in which we take participants through building a full Ember.js application to advanced workshops on techniques like composition of complex component hierarchies and Data Down, Actions Up, FastBoot, performance optimizing applications etc.
